WHO WE ARE ?

Mumin International is a Manpower Consultancy which holds the recruitment license issued in Bangladesh in 2021 to any overseas recruitment company in Bangladesh. Mumin is Bangladesh's pioneering recruitment organisation with having placed 350,000 candidates in over 10 countries worldwide. Over the years we have been serving as a catalyst in bringing together career seekers and employers from across the world. We have been instrumental in placing unskilled, semi skilled, skilled and highly skilled personnel in the Oil & Gas, Construction, Maintenance, Trading and various other sectors globally.
